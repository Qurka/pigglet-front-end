Piggylet is a DeFi protocol for peer-to-peer loan transactions that enables NFT holders to access liquidity without selling their NFTs. Additionally, it allows DeFi investors to earn yield through lending secured by NFT assets. With its NFT classification system and safe lending/borrowing features via the liquidation mechanism, Piggylet makes it possible for the majority of NFTs to benefit from NFT-Backed Lending solutions.

Borrowers do not need to sell their NFTs for liquidity needs. Instead, they can collateralize their NFTs to obtain loans from lenders and fulfill their liquidity needs. Lenders may evaluate their idle capital by lending to borrowers and earning a high annual percentage rate (APR).

Borrowers are platform users who use their NFTs as collateral to obtain a loan from lenders. NFTs are transferred to an escrow smart contract, and when borrowers pay their loans back within the agreed-upon terms, they may claim their NFTs back.
